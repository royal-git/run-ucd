RÁS UCD

Run for a cause

(and amazing goodies!)

The RÁS UCD is an annual 5k road race that takes place in UCD. It is a bilingual event and a fun filled day for the whole family!

Make your training and racing more meaningful by raising awareness and funds to support UCD Volunteers Overseas Projects! The event is open to all.

Sign up today!

You can sign up online and the fee is only €12 for students and €15 for everyone else. Be one of our first ticket buyers and you could win a free t-shirt and goodey bag!

There are plenty of prizes and refreshments!
