Group Runs

Along with the daily run from 5-6 every evening, we conduct group runs throughout the week. We meet most saturdays at 6am during the summer and 5pm during the winters.

Tuesday Tracks

Meet members at the UCD running track every tuesday at five for some intense running sessions. Great place to meet new people as this is more of a social event than a competiton.

Annual family gathering

We have a huge barbecue feast every year for your whole family. It's a day of fun and excitement. Meet new people and raise money for UCDVO. Last year's family feast was a huge success and we were able to raise €5,620 for the organisation. We're hoping to continue this tradition as we continue to become a bigger and better club.
