LIFE IS A SPORT. MAKE IT COUNT.

Care to join us for a run?

We practice every evening from 5-6. We begin outside the UCD Sports Centre. Everyone is welcome from students, teachers and the public! We're flexible and we do runs all through the day with members showing up and planning runs through our mobile app! We promise that if you do decide to join us, you'll be coming into an environment that will both inspire you to run and keep you fit.
